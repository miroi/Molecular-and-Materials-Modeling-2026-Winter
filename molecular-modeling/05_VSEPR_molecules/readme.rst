=====
VSEPR
=====

Geometry optimization of selected toy molecules with NWChem and MOPAC.

Checking whether final geometries satisfy VSEPR geometry.

See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VSEPR_theory


Challenge
---------
Carry out geometry investigation (Avogadro2, MOPAC, NWChem) also for other molecules - BeF2, BCl3, PF5.
